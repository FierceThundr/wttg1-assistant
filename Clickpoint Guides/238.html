We conduct a wide range of experiments on human subjects at our facilities. The people chosen for these experiments are usually among the homeless, unregistered citizens, and we have recently secured a backchannel for obtaining members of various correctional systems. This allows us to spin up large scale tests very quickly.

Experiments

- starvation and water/fluid restriction
- vivisection / pain tolerances
- infectious diseases and organ effects
- transfusions
- drug trials
- sterilization
- neonate and infant tolerances to x-rays, heat and pressure.
- transplantation
- radiation exposure
- conscious and unconscious testing

Lab Work

Laboratory examinations (full blood counts, urinalysis, chemistries… etc) are done off-site at hospitals where we take the samples and send them under other current patients’ names. The results are carefully dispersed so as not to arouse suspicion.

In cases of death

The bodies of the dead are now cremated on site using Alkaline Hydrolysis at warehouse 4.

Expedited trials and testing

IRB approval is never sought for the experiments performed at our facilities. Our ability to acquire test subjects at such a rapid rate allows the ability to spin up large scale testing quickly.
