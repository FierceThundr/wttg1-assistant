WALL THREE OF INTERIUS

The void of emotion within yourself. When mastered, you have become grey. When you have managed to shut yourself off from the emotions that pull us to irrationality and can no longer be moved by others meaningless lives, you have emptied your center and may live in peace which, in turn, will give peace to others for eternity.

When you can live your life removed from all. Numb to the zeitgeist of our world. You can begin to help others return the world to balance. You understand that many can simply not live in the shade. When you are grey, you poses an ability others simply do not have. The ability to not be harmed. Invincible to the petty nothingness that is the lives of others.

Their needs and wants no longer hold meaning and the absence of their presence brings more balance to your world and the world around you. When you understand that those within the hues of the dizzying world are simply selfish and feeding off of the life force of others, it is simple to remove them.

Their lives do not belong in the grey world. A world where all exist in peace where all can be who they truly are. Who you are. You are special. You are strong. You are in the shade that others can never enter. They will burn in the bright rays of emotion for their judgments upon others. Your house is built and they may never enter it's sacred walls. They will plead. They will scream. They will die as they are meant to die just as they are meant to live. Let them die as they are no importance.

Their lives are short. They provide no benefit to your world. They are a cancer for the peace of all and they will be removed. You and the many links in the chain of grey will pull them away from their selfish lives. When their is void there is peace.

Go into the shade now. Your house is built.
